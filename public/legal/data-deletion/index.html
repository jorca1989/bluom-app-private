Data Deletion & Account Removal

Last updated: January 8, 2025

🗑️ Your Right to be Forgotten

You have the right to request permanent deletion of all your personal data from Bluom. This includes all health data, activity logs, preferences, and account information.

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • • User profile and authentication data
  • • All fasting logs and metabolic data
  • • Hormonal cycle tracking data
  • • Exercise and activity history
  • • Sleep and wellness logs
  • • Food and nutrition entries
  • • Subscription and payment records
  • • App preferences and settings
  • • AI coaching conversations

⏱️ Deletion Process

  1. Submit Request: Fill out the form below with your registered email
  2. Verification: We verify your identity using the email on file
  3. Processing: Your deletion request is processed within 24 hours
  4. Confirmation: You'll receive an email confirmation when deletion is complete
  5. Permanent: This action cannot be undone - all data is permanently erased

⚠️ Permanent Data Deletion Request

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be lost forever.

📧 Need Help?

If you have trouble with the deletion process or need assistance:

Email: support@bluom.app

Subject: "Data Deletion Request"

Response time: Within 24 hours
