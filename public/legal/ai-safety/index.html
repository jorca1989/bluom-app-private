AI Safety & Responsibility

🤖 AI Agent Disclaimer

Our AI Agent provides suggestions based on your logged data. It is not a doctor.

The "AI Coach", "Fuel Vision", and other automated insights are probabilistic tools designed to assist with lifestyle organization. They can make errors.

Users should verify all nutritional and fitness advice before implementation.

🧠 How it Works

The AI analyzes patterns in your logged food, exercise, and biometric data to suggest optimizations (e.g., "You might sleep better if you eat earlier").

  • It does not diagnose medical conditions.
  • It does not prescribe treatments.
  • It creates suggestions based on general wellness principles.

🔒 AI & Your Data

We prioritize your privacy in our AI implementation:

  • No Public Training: We do not use your personal health data to train public AI models (like ChatGPT).
  • Personal Context: Your data is used only to generate insights for you.
