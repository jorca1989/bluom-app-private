Data Privacy & Biological Security

The "Life Management" Edition

We treat your data like a biological bank account. This includes your Fuel logs (food/calories), Move history (exercise logs), Wellness data (mood/sleep), and Biometric Blueprint (cycle/metabolic phases).

🚫 Zero-Sale Policy

We do not sell your data. Period.

🛡️ Infrastructure

We use world-class, encrypted infrastructure to ensure your data is never accessible by unauthorized parties. We partner only with top-tier secure infrastructure providers.

🤖 AI Processing

Our AI Coach and Calorie Detector process data to give you personalized insights. We do not use your personal data to train public AI models.

Shop & Recommendations

Used to maintain your Fasting Timer and Activity Logs even when the app is closed.
