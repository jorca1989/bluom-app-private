💳 Billing Information

🔄 Auto-Renewal

Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. You'll receive a reminder email before renewal.

💳 Payment Methods

We accept payment through your app store (Google Play Store or Apple App Store). All payment processing is handled securely by your app store.

📧 Billing Receipts

Receipts are sent to your app store email address automatically after each payment.

💰 Refund Policy

🏪 App Store Refunds

All refunds are handled through your app store (Google Play or Apple App Store). Bluom does not process refunds directly.

⏰ Refund Timeframes

  • Google Play: 48 hours for automatic approval, up to 3 business days for manual review
  • Apple App Store: Typically processed within 24-48 hours

📧 Need Billing Help?

For billing issues not resolved through your app store:

Email: billing@bluom.app

Include: Your email, purchase date, and issue description

Response time: Within 24-48 hours
